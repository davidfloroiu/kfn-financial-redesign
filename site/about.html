01 The firm KFN — Accounting & Financial
Our story

Built to restore clarity and integrity to modern business.

Numbers tell part of the story — but your financial situation is deeply connected to your life, your business, and your future plans. So we built KFN to give individuals and growing businesses the caliber of counsel usually reserved for large enterprises: we listen first, then craft the strategy around you.

Our mission is simple — help you keep more of what you earn, make smarter financial choices, and build the future you envision. That means minimizing tax liability while ensuring full compliance, keeping your financials accurate and current, and putting a seasoned financial voice at your table before every big decision.

03 Leadership KFN — Accounting & Financial
KN

Kyle F. Nader, CPA, CFE.

Founder & Managing Partner. Kyle spent four years in RSM's financial advisory practice working on complex litigation support, forensic accounting, fraud investigations, financial modeling, and business valuation — then three years at Infor consulting on financial and supply chain software, before stepping into the CFO seat at an e-commerce manufacturer.

He founded KFN to bring that enterprise-caliber experience to individuals, startups, and small-to-medium businesses — tailored counsel across accounting, tax, and business consulting, delivered personally and in plain English.
