02 Scope of Work KFN — Accounting & Financial

Two kinds of taxpayer. One standard of care.

Whether it's a personal return with income in three states or an entity return with quarterly estimates, the engagement is the same: CPAs, not software alone.

Individuals & Families

Personal returns handled end to end — with planning that starts well before the deadline, not the week of it.

  • Income tax preparationFederal, state, and local returns — accurate, complete, and filed on time.
  • Multi-state filingsMoved, worked remotely, or earned income across state lines — covered.
  • Amended returnsMissed deductions and filing errors corrected, with penalties minimized.
  • Year-round tax planningDeductions, credits, and the timing of income and expenses — planned in advance.

Businesses & Entities

Business tax as a discipline, not a deadline — compliance handled and strategy built into the calendar.

  • Business & entity returnsFederal, state, and local business filings, prepared and reviewed by CPAs.
  • Strategic tax planningStructure, elections, and timing that minimize tax liability — legally and deliberately.
  • SALT & multi-state complianceIncome, sales, and corporate taxes in every state you operate in.
  • Quarterly estimatesCalculated, calendared, and paid on schedule — nothing sneaks up on you.
  • 1099 preparation & filingContractors documented and forms filed on time, every January.
