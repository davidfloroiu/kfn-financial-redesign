01 The mandate KFN — Accounting & Financial

One office for everything you own.

High-net-worth clients require a trusted partner to manage the complexities of their financial and business affairs. Our private business management practice goes beyond traditional accounting — from advanced tax planning and preparation to strategic business consulting — working to optimize financial performance, mitigate risk, and safeguard your wealth and business interests for long-term growth.

The aim is straightforward: build wealth, minimize tax liability, and unlock growth — with clear financial oversight behind every decision and comprehensive support at every step.

In plain terms
One office quietly manages the financial side of everything you own — the entities, the taxes, the cash, the deals — and answers only to you.
02 Main responsibilities KFN — Accounting & Financial

Six areas. One accountable office.

Every engagement is tailored, but the mandate typically spans six standing responsibilities — each handled quietly, each reported plainly.

  • 01
    Multi-entity structures & complex tax strategy Year-round, proactive tax strategy across every entity you hold. Holdings structured for optimal tax treatment, a standing focus on minimizing tax liability, and personal, corporate, and multi-state returns prepared.
  • 02
    Cash flow & financial oversight Accurate, real-time bookkeeping across all entities; monthly financial reviews with variance analysis; budgets, forecasts, and cash-flow models — income, expenses, and vendor payments tracked continuously.
  • 03
    Vendor & lender relationships The financial relationships around your ventures, managed — coordination with lenders on compliance and financing needs, vendor obligations kept current, and the documentation partners and potential buyers expect maintained.
  • 04
    Family-office operations The administrative backbone of a family office, without the overhead of building one — consolidated reporting across ventures and households, ongoing financial administration, and key performance indicators monitored and reported.
  • 05
    Deal support & investor relations New opportunities sourced, vetted, and analyzed; financial due diligence and feasibility assessments; valuation guidance and exit planning; support for investor communications, reporting, and the structuring of deals.
  • 06
    Asset protection & risk management Financial operations kept aligned with legal and tax compliance; insurance policies, contracts, and liabilities reviewed across ventures; internal controls monitored, with fraud-prevention counsel from a Certified Fraud Examiner.
