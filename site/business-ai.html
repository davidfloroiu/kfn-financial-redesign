01 The platform KFN — Accounting & Financial
What it is

The machine finds it. A CPA verifies it

Most owners don't need more reports — they need answers. SteveKnows analyzes the financials you already keep and surfaces what matters: valuation, forecasts, tax-saving opportunities, and how you stack up against businesses like yours.

And because it lives inside a CPA firm, no finding stands on machine output alone. A KFN CPA reviews the analysis, confirms what holds up, and walks you through what it means — in plain English.

02 Capabilities KFN — Accounting & Financial

The questions owners actually ask

Every capability in SteveKnows exists to answer a question you've already asked yourself.

And behind every answer sits a CFO-level reading of the results — what the numbers mean, where the pressure is building, and what's worth doing about it now.

Bring your own question
  • 01
    What is my business worth today?Business valuation grounded in your actual financials — earnings, assets, and cash flow — rather than a rule-of-thumb multiple overheard at a dinner party.
  • 02
    Where do revenue and cash flow go from here?Forward-looking forecasts of revenue and cash position, so the next quarter is a plan you've seen — not a surprise you absorb.
  • 03
    What tax savings am I missing?Tax-savings discovery scans your financials for opportunities to minimize liability — and a KFN CPA confirms each one before you act on it.
  • 04
    How do I compare with businesses like mine?Benchmarking against comparable businesses, so you know which numbers deserve pride — and which deserve attention.
