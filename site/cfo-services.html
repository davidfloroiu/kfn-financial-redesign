03 When to hire KFN — Accounting & Financial

Three signs it's time for financial leadership.

01 You've outgrown bookkeeping The books are current and the filings are on time — but no one is telling you what the numbers mean for next quarter's decisions. You need answers, not just records.
02 You're raising capital Lenders and investors expect forecasts, models, and financial statements that hold up to scrutiny — built and defended by someone who has done it before. Numbers that survive due diligence.
03 You're planning an exit A strong sale is won long before the closing table — clean financials, efficient systems, and a business that can run without you. Get sale-ready before you need to be.
