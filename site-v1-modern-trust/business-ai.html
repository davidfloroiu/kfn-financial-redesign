In Plain Terms

SteveKnows reads your books and answers the questions you'd ask a CFO — then a CPA checks the answer before it reaches you.

What It Is

The machine finds it. A CPA verifies it.

Most owners don't need more reports — they need answers. SteveKnows analyzes the financials you already keep and surfaces what matters: valuation, forecasts, tax-saving opportunities, and how you stack up against businesses like yours.

And because it lives inside a CPA firm, no finding stands on machine output alone. A KFN CPA reviews the analysis, confirms what holds up, and walks you through what it means — in plain English.

Five Capabilities

The questions owners actually ask.

Every capability in SteveKnows exists to answer a question you've already asked yourself.

I.

What is my business worth?

A valuation grounded in your actual financials — earnings, assets, and cash flow — rather than a rule-of-thumb multiple overheard at a dinner party.

Capability: business valuation
II.

Where do revenue and cash flow go from here?

Forward-looking forecasts of revenue and cash position, so the next quarter is a plan you've seen — not a surprise you absorb.

Capability: revenue & cash-flow forecasting
III.

What tax savings am I missing?

SteveKnows scans your financials for opportunities to minimize tax liability — and a KFN CPA confirms each one before you act on it.

Capability: tax-savings discovery
IV.

How do I compare with businesses like mine?

Benchmarks your performance against comparable businesses, so you know which numbers deserve pride — and which deserve attention.

Capability: benchmarking
V.

What should I do next?

A CFO-level reading of the results: what the numbers mean, where the pressure is building, and what's worth doing about it now.

Capability: CFO-level insights
