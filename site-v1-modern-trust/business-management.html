Main Responsibilities

Six areas. One accountable office.

Every engagement is tailored, but the mandate typically spans six standing responsibilities — each handled quietly, each reported plainly.

i.

Multi-Entity Structures & Complex Tax Strategy

Year-round, proactive tax strategy across every entity you hold. We structure holdings for optimal tax treatment, focus on minimizing tax liability, and prepare personal, corporate, and multi-state returns.

In plain English: every entity, one strategy — filed right, everywhere.
ii.

Cash Flow & Financial Oversight

Accurate, real-time bookkeeping across all entities; monthly financial reviews with variance analysis; budgets, forecasts, and cash-flow models — income, expenses, and vendor payments tracked continuously.

In plain English: you always know where the money stands.
iii.

Vendor & Lender Relationships

We manage the financial relationships around your ventures — coordinating with lenders on compliance and financing needs, keeping vendor obligations current, and maintaining the documentation partners and potential buyers expect.

In plain English: we make the calls you shouldn't have to.
iv.

Family-Office Operations

The administrative backbone of a family office, without the overhead of building one — consolidated reporting across ventures and households, ongoing financial administration, and key performance indicators monitored and reported.

In plain English: family-office order, without the family office.
v.

Deal Support & Investor Relations

We source, vet, and analyze new opportunities; conduct financial due diligence and feasibility assessments; provide valuation guidance and exit planning; and support investor communications, reporting, and the structuring of deals.

In plain English: a numbers partner at every table.
vi.

Asset Protection & Risk Management

Financial operations kept aligned with legal and tax compliance; insurance policies, contracts, and liabilities reviewed across ventures; internal controls monitored, with fraud-prevention counsel from a Certified Fraud Examiner.

In plain English: protection arranged before it's needed.
